Communication processes

WhatsApp
Many owners and residents are members of the Northmarque community chat on WhatsApp. This chat is not operated by the Body Corporate or the committee and is not an official communication channel.
For any body corporate questions or support, the easiest way for owners and residents to contact us is by emailing community@northmarque.net or one of the other email addresses listed below, rather than using WhatsApp.
BC Systems and Body Corporate Manager communication
We’re pleased to let you know that the transition to BC Systems is going smoothly, and we expect to be ready to hold our Annual General Meeting in January 2026. In the meantime, we have now confirmed how owners and residents can get in touch with BC Systems for all body corporate matters.
Staying in touch
To keep things simple, owners and residents can use the same details they already use for any enquiry or support request. The main email address is community@northmarque.net and there are additional options for different types of requests listed below. ​
Why this helps everyone
Using this process means your message will reach the right person more quickly and can be tracked from start to finish. It also supports good governance and record‑keeping for the body corporate and helps keep management costs down for all owners.
No need to copy multiple emails
There is no need to copy in multiple addresses or send the same message more than once. Sending your enquiry to the one correct contact is enough for it to be directed where it needs to go and responded to as promptly as possible.
Communication guide
Use the email addresses below. The examples of common enquiries can help you choose the best one, but don’t worry – just pick the address that seems right and your message will still get where it needs to go.
