Heatwave warning

Dear residents

The Bureau of Meteorology has issued a heatwave warning for our area between Wednesday 11 February 2026 and Saturday 14 February 2026.

Maximum temperatures in the low to mid thirties, with overnight minimum temperatures in the low to mid twenties. Severe to locally extreme heatwave conditions are currently developing.

Severe heatwave conditions are expected to peak late in the week before easing on Sunday.

For more information:

Warning page: The Bureau of Meteorology

‍

Severe heatwaves can be dangerous for many people. Seek a place to keep cool, such as your home, a library, community centre or shopping centre.
